Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your development process?

We follow a structured 4-phase process: Discovery & Planning, Design & Wireframing, Development & Testing, and Launch & Optimization. You'll receive regular updates throughout each phase.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, updated, and running smoothly. Our support includes regular backups, security monitoring, and content updates.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We have experience integrating with various existing systems, APIs, and databases. We'll analyze your current setup and recommend the best integration approach.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, PHP, Python, and cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud. We choose the best tech stack for each project.

Do you offer training?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training sessions and documentation to help your team manage and update your website independently. We also offer ongoing consultation as needed.
